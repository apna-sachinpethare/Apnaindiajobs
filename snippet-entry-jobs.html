Snippet Entry Jobs Work From Home

We are looking for a good Indian typist who will be responsible for typing the company's snippet entry documents and material, reports, formulas, and policies. If you can type fast with good accuracy and can check documents for grammar, spelling, and punctuation errors under strict deadlines, we’d like to hire you. For this role, it’s important to handle sensitive information with confidentiality. Ultimately, you should be able to ensure our company/client's documents are accurate and updated. Below are the details of snippet entry jobs.

  • Snippet entry jobs are a process of typing a small piece of content that contains one to two sentences with 3 to 4 lines by using the MS-Word application.

  • In snippet entry jobs, you will earn 3/- rupees per document. Please note that we only pay for right entries and for wrong entries we don't deduct any money from your earnings.

  • There is no minimum payout for snippet entry work. After you completed all your work and at the end of your plan validity date, we will transfer your all earnings to your bank account or UPI account.

  • The payment cycle for snippet entry work is monthly, You will get all your earnings monthly after you completed your work.

  • You will receive your payout or earnings directly to your bank account or UPI account whichever is convenient for you. Please note that you can also provide your parent's bank account for payout.

  • In this job, you will receive a total of 1,500 documents per month which means you will receive 50 documents per day.

  • In snippet entry jobs, you can earn ₹4,500/- per month.

  • Below are the demos or samples of snippet entry jobs. Please see the demo carefully before you pay the security deposit amount and start your work.

  • You can register on our website for free but to start your work with us, you have to pay the security deposit charges.

  • The validity of snippet entry work is for 1 month after a month if we like your work and accuracy then you don't have to pay us any money for any work. We will provide you our typing jobs for FREE!

  • We will provide you with scanned typing documents on a daily basis, You just have to type the document on the MS word application and you will earn Rs. 3/- for each right entry.

  • You have to complete all your assignment or documents if you want to request for your payout.

  • Click on the "Get Job" button and pay the security deposit charges of the snippet entry jobs to start working and earning with us.

  • Note: Like other genuine and real data entry companies, Once your work is done and you have completed all your work and submitted the work, we'll refund your security deposit charges along with your monthly payout. We get security deposit fees because we want you to work with us seriously and complete your work with honesty and accuracy. We are a third-party data entry website and if you are not able to complete your work on time then it's a loss for both of us. Stay Safe!
